### Three Levers That Matter

Every enterprise AI deployment ultimately optimises across three levers — **Customer Experience**, **Revenue**, and **Cost**. The companies winning right now are the ones deploying agents across all three simultaneously, not just one.

**Experience:** Agents that provide instant product discovery, live stock visibility, and order tracking — replacing the "call your rep and wait" cycle with real-time, conversational interactions.

**Revenue:** 24×7 agent-led ordering, intelligent reordering and upsell using purchase history, scheme-led buying nudges with progress tracking, and alternate inventory discovery. The result: no monetisation leaks from missed orders, under-utilised schemes, or rep unavailability.

**Cost:** The 70/20/10 split is emerging as the new operating model — 70% of interactions handled entirely by AI (FAQs, order status, stock checks, invoice generation), 20% human-assisted with AI doing the prep work (complaints, complex responses), and only 10% requiring full human involvement (crisis management, high-stakes VIP sales). That's a structural shift in cost-per-interaction.

These are the agent types we're seeing move from pilot to production across India and Southeast Asia — and the ones worth thinking about for your business:

**Primary & Secondary Sales**
- Distributor ordering: from calls and complex portals → 24×7 conversational ordering with cross-warehouse inventory, auto pricing, credit validation, and instant ERP entry
- Retailer ordering: from FA-dependent manual process → live inventory across distributors, scheme application, instant booking

**Scheme & Target Acceleration**
- Real-time scheme tracking, progress nudges ("You're 40 units away from the target"), auto top-up booking — replacing manual scheme communication and under-utilised incentives

**Secondary Sales Data Collection**
- Field agents: invoice photo on WhatsApp → AI extraction → instant ERP update. What currently takes 5–7 days of manual entry and back-office review now takes minutes

**Replenishment Intelligence**
- AI predicts demand based on velocity and history, sends proactive reorder alerts — reducing stock-outs and capturing latent demand

**Order Status & Financial Queries**
- "Where is my order?" gets an instant response. Ledger, invoices, credit status auto-shared — no more calls to support

**Customer Support & Complaint Routing**
- AI logs, classifies, and routes with full context. Humans handle only high-stakes cases. Post-sale queries, warranty, complaint tracking — 24×7, in regional languages

**Product Discovery & Lead Gen**
- For large catalogues (60K+ SKUs), agents that understand specs, compatibility, and customer context. On brand websites, qualifying and routing leads in real-time

**Data Analyst**
- Ask your data questions in natural language, get answers across all channels and markets

The pattern: connect to the customer where they already are (WhatsApp, voice, website), connect to the back-end where the data lives (SAP, ERP, CRM, DMS) — and let the agent handle the loop.

**The transformation in numbers:** A standard distributor order today involves personal WhatsApp messages, phone calls, 1–4 days, and multiple humans. With an agentic workflow: a single brand-owned WhatsApp number, ERP-integrated, 10–15 minutes, minimal human intervention. Field agent invoice processing: from 5–6 days of manual data entry → 3–5 minutes with AI extraction.

### Measuring What Matters

One question we get often: "How do I know if agents are actually working?" Here's the scorecard framework we use:

- **Experience Leader** — Customer Effort Score (CES), CSAT, First Contact Resolution, Self-Serve Adoption
- **Revenue Leader** — Conversion Lift (CVR), Engagement Rate, Order Volume Growth, Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- **Cost Leader** — Cost Per Interaction (CPI), Average Handling Time (AHT), Turnaround Time (TAT), Total Service Cost Reduction

The hero metric depends on what you're solving for first. But the compounding effect — better experience drives higher revenue at lower cost — is where the real ROI shows up.
